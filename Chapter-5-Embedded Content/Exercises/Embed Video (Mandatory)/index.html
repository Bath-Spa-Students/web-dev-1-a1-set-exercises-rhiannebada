ENHYPEN (엔하이픈) 'Sweet Venom' Official MV



ENHYPEN is a boyband created by BELIFT LAB, consisting of seven muntinational members JUNGWON, HEESEUNG, JAY, JAKE, SUNGHOON, SUNOO, and NI-KI.

Inspired by the 'hyphen(-)' that connects one word to another, ENHYPEN means that seven different boys connect to discover one another and grow together. They also have an ambitious plan to connect people and the world through their music.
